About

Louis Antonio Suarez is an Architecture student at Carnegie Mellon University. His work reflects a sensibility to highly crafted images that use both analogue and digital techniques to create moving experiential spaces.

Louis is also a lover of classical music. He held the position of First Guitar in the Pittsburgh Classical Guitar Society Orchestra for two years before joining the Carnegie Mellon Universty Guitar Ensemble.

Louis is also interested in screenwriting. He has written three short screenplays and is currently working on a fourth. His screenplay "Winterreise" won first place in the 2016 Ralph Munn Creative Writing Contest.
